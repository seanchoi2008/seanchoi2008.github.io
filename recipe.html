Ham and Cheese Sandwich

  • Any type of flatbread
  • Any type of ham
  • Any type of cheese

This recipe is for a ham sandwich. It is simple to make, if you have the right ingredients.

  1. Put the flatbread on a table
  2. Put ham on the flatbread
  3. Then, add the cheese
  4. With your other peice of flatbread, put it ontop
